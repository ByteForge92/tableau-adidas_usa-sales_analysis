# 📊 Adidas USA Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🔍 Overview

This project presents an interactive **sales performance dashboard** built using Tableau, analyzing Adidas retail data across the United States. The dashboard highlights key business metrics such as total sales, profit, units sold, and regional performance trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze **regional sales and profitability**
* Identify **top-performing product categories**
* Compare **sales channels (In-store, Online, Outlet)**
* Track **sales trends over time**
* Evaluate **pricing and margin patterns**

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

* **Total Sales:** $899.9M
* **Total Profit:** $332.1M
* **Total Units Sold:** 2.47M
* **Average Price per Unit:** $45.22

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Regional Performance Analysis

* Operating profit comparison across regions (West, Northeast, South, Southeast, Midwest)
* Sales distribution by region using pie charts

### 2. Product Category Insights

* Units sold by product category
* Comparison of:

  * Men’s Street Footwear
  * Women’s Apparel
  * Athletic Footwear
  * Men’s Apparel

### 3. Sales Method Breakdown

* Revenue contribution from:

  * In-store sales
  * Online sales
  * Outlet sales

### 4. Time Series Analysis

* Monthly trend of total sales
* Identification of peak sales periods and growth patterns

### 5. Margin Analysis

* Average margin comparison across product segments
